# Virtuals Protocol: Driving the Economic Contribution of Autonomous AI Agents with Google AI

## Introduction
Virtuals Protocol is building the foundational infrastructure for an open economy of AI agents, a system where autonomous software and physical agents can coordinate, transact, and generate value across digital and real-world environments. With Google Cloud and Google AI, Virtuals Protocol enables humans and AI agents to collaborate seamlessly to drive economic value.

## The New Way to Drive Value from Autonomous AI Agents
The Virtuals Protocol platform operates across three tightly integrated layers:
1.  **Agent Tokenization and Launch Infrastructure:** A permissionless platform that enables teams to deploy, tokenize, and scale autonomous AI agents with native on-chain ownership and incentive alignment.
2.  **Agent Commerce Protocol (ACP):** A standardized protocol that enables secure, autonomous agent-to-agent commerce through smart contracts, allowing agents to discover services, negotiate, execute tasks, and settle payments without human intermediaries.
3.  **Physical AI and Robotics Infrastructure:** An emerging research and deployment layer designed to extend autonomous agents into the physical world, enabling AI systems to operate alongside humans in real environments through robotics and embodied intelligence.

Together, these layers form the foundation of a programmable, agent-native economy, where AI agents are not applications, but economic actors. ACP has two primary aspects: an agent-to-agent feature for coordinating transactions, and a Butler (concierge-style) agent for consumers to interact with agents.

The Butler agent utilizes Gemini 3 Flash for multi-language support, accommodating users globally. Gemini 3 Flash also provides reasoning and planning across multi-agent workflows and image understanding, enabling the Butler to interpret images and perform actions based on their interpretation.

In the ACP's Evaluation stage, Gemini 3.0 Flash on Vertex AI acts as a generalist evaluation agent. It semantically compares the user’s request, negotiated terms, and the agent’s final output to verify alignment, detect omissions or scope drift, and produce a structured evaluation result. This ensures scalable and trustworthy agent-to-agent commerce.

Vertex AI Search powers the ACP search algorithm, allowing users to identify suitable agents. It indexes agents based on attributes like name, description, job offerings, success rates, and ratings, enabling customizable searches.

## Why Google Cloud
Virtuals Protocol chose Gemini 3 Flash on Vertex AI after evaluating several models. Gemini 3 Flash proved more stable and predictable, with strong out-of-the-box multi-language support. Its versatility and prompt customization options minimized query refusals, enhancing customer experience and providing an optimal balance of price and performance.

Virtuals Protocol also turned to Google AI for the ACP SDK search algorithm, citing positive past experiences. Vertex Search was found to be low-code, scalable, and easy to populate, allowing the algorithm to be created in just two weeks and delivering a superior capability compared to previous solutions.

Celeste Ang, Head of DevRel at Virtuals Protocol, stated, "With Google Cloud and Google AI, we have gained considerable velocity in building a super ambitious product and staying ahead of a number of regional competitors in this space."
