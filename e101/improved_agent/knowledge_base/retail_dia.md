# Case Study: Dia - A new way to reach more homes with the power of the cloud

## Overview
Dia, a leader in proximity distribution in Spain, aimed to expand its digital reach to offer a more agile shopping experience adapted to current needs. This digital evolution responded directly to a 40% increase in mobile shopping.

## Challenge
Dia wanted to transfer its physical proximity (60% of the population within a 15-minute walk) to the online environment. The strategic goal was to reach more homes with the fastest shopping experience in the market, optimizing delivery times to less than an hour. The existing infrastructure lacked the scalability and flexibility needed for expansion.

## Solution
Dia chose Google Cloud for its unified omnichannel platform. The architecture is native cloud based on microservices and rapid deployments using Kubernetes.

### Key Google Cloud Products Used:
- **Google Kubernetes Engine (GKE)**
- **BigQuery** (used for data analytics and insights)
- **Dataflow**
- **Cloud Composer**
- **Dataproc**
- **Cloud SQL**
- **Apigee X**
- **Cloud Operations**
- **Google Cloud Storage**

## Results
- **Agile deployment:** Platform launched in 10 months.
- **Increased coverage:** Online coverage expanded from 52% to 84% of the Spanish population (from 22.7 million to 36.5 million inhabitants).
- **Operational efficiency:** 15% increase in complete orders (from 75% to 90%) and 25% improvement in quality of preparation and delivery between 2023 and 2024.
- **Customer growth:** 25% increase in ecommerce customers and 50% increase in omnichannel customers.
- **Omnichannel experience:** Delivery within 1-hour time slots and real-time order tracking.

> "Omnichannel allows us to better fulfill our promise of proximity. Today you can place an order and receive it in less than an hour. That is also proximity."
> — Máximo Ventas de la Cruz, Global CTO & CIO, Dia
